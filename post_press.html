HUMAN CHAIN TO CREATE VOTE AWARENESS

New Delhi, 5th may 2021 TIAS succeeded in making a human chain virtually with the help of 400 students and college
members in order to create vote awareness on 4th may 2021. A voter awareness campaign, emphasizing the
importance of voting was held on zoom platform by to make sure that the voters exercise their democratic right.
Students painted national flag and vote sign on their faces and other activities like students displayed placards with
motivational statements written on them to sensitize voters about the importance of voting.

On this occasion of voter awareness programme college teaching staff,non- teaching staff, NSS unit also took part.
The cumulated majority of students emphasized on importance of voting and elections and asked everyone to go
to their voting booth on the election day. Our honourable Director Dr. Ajay kumar motivated everyone to go vote on the
voting day because only through this system we can have a strong democracy. He also stated that “ in order to create
awareness and bring change it is the youth’s input,which will bring the major change in this initiative”. It is necessary
for everyone to vote in order to make a change in our country. Honourable Dean Dr. MN Jha appreciated this
moment created by the students and college staff, as this initiative will motivate people for coming state elections.
With help of this,people can vote for the right leader. After seeing the turn-up number of total college member for the
human chain activity, administrative people appreciated TIAS initiative .
